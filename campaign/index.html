Passengers often wait at bus stops without knowing when the bus will arrive, leading to long waits and sometimes choosing more expensive transport options. To address this, a mobile app could be developed, connecting to bus conductors' ticket machines via GPS. The app would provide real-time bus locations and available seats based on ticket data, reducing overcrowding at stops and enhancing the commuter experience. Just as we now have apps for tracking train status, a similar solution could greatly benefit public bus commuters, who number in the millions.
